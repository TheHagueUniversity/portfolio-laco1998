hallmARk

Bringing digital gift unwrapping closer to the real one is the goal of this app/game. It is an AR experience suited mainly for football and gaming fans. In order to get to game, the player has to receive a Hallmark card. After downloading the hallmARk and scanning the card, the player is brought into an AR football game. After successfully completing the game, there is a gift waiting for the player. This is not a fully functional app, it is just a prototype.

Creating postcards seems old-fashioned nowadays, but it does not have to be. Shelf

Diverging and converging is a crucial part of a work of a designer, but it is not always easy to remember all the techniques and methods. This app makes life of a designer much easier. In this app users can find many converging and diverging techniques, also with instructions, rules, and possible results. Coming up with new ideas have never been easier thanks to the Shelf. This is not a fully functional app, it is just a prototype.
